Our Mission

Turathi bridges the gap between the past and the future. We use VR reconstruction, 3D scanning, and historical storytelling to revive Algeria’s cultural heritage. Our goal is to let anyone, anywhere, walk through ancient sites as they once were: vivid, immersive, and alive. Founded in 2025, our multidisciplinary team of VR developers, historians, and artists work hand-in-hand to safeguard the country’s treasures digitally ensuring they remain accessible for generations to come..
